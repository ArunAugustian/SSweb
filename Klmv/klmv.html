66kV SubStation, Kulamavu under Thodupuzha Division. Power tapped from moolamattom-Vazhathope line and 6.6 mVA Transformer placed in Substation along with 3 output Feeders, Colony,Dam and Vadakepuzha(dedicated feeder for Vadakepuzha pumb house). and also have BVC and Axillary feeders. BVC for Back feeding from moolamattom substation and Auxillary for station power supply.
